Nu Upsilon Tau

Honorary Tea Fraternity

The honorary tea organization Nu Upsilon Tau is dedicated to promoting the most ideal human characteristics known to mankind. The founding Alpha Chapter at UCLA was erected in the wake of the overwhelming desire to honor these characteristics. Our members are carefully selected through an intensive candidate process in which they prove their allegiance to these invaluable human qualities. Once initiated, our members devote their life to upholding the ideals of our fraternity while encouraging the spirit of Nu Upsilon Tau in others.
